Product Design at Wave

I lead Product Design at Wave to send and save money, pay bills, take payments, and more. I'm so excited to be a part of Wave, which is one of the fastest growing fintechs in Africa.

It's an exciting time to join the growing design team at Wave: we're building in new markets, launching new products, and figuring out how to scale. Here are some things I do with our amazing design team.

Design that makes a difference

Wave enables users to send and receive payments for free in countries like Senegal, Ivory Coast, Uganda, and more. If you work on Wave's product design team, you'll be creating design that makes real differences in people's lives.

Customers use Wave to make their lives easier and their businesses grow. Wave enables them to pay school fees, pay for groceries, send money to their family, and accept customer payments. I'm inspired by the real impact Wave has on customers daily.

Visual and UI style

I lead our small but nimble design team. Together, we ship impactful user experience improvements that make the app easier to understand and use.

We do our best to review constraints related to connectivity, phone types, screen sizes, and literacy levels. We find the best solutions and build a design system that scales across our expanding set of products.

Tight loop of design & research

I work closely with our research team to understand user needs and validate solutions. We run usability tests, field research, and quantitative studies to inform our design decisions.

Depending on the project, we make Figma prototypes for usability testing. For many products we test with 5+ users before launch with our talented user research team.
