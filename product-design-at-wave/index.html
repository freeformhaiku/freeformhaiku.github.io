Tight loop of design & research

Product Designers work closely with PMs and User Researchers to create products that customers will use and understand.

User Researchers play an essential part of the product process. They help product teams test prototypes, gather context, and gain a better understanding of customer’s habits and expectations.

Depending on the project, we create Figma or vibecoded prototypes that have just enough interactive fidelity for User Researchers to test with real customers. Then we iterate based on feedback from customers.
